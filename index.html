About

I am an undergraduate research intern at Universal Transfer Learning Laboratory (UTL Lab) under the supervision of Prof. Donghyun Kim
My research interests are vision-language model(VLM) especially in visual/video question answering(VQA) and embodied AI.
Previously, I was a research intern at MLV lab, advised by Hyunwoo J, Kim, and taught by Jongha Kim and Jinyoung Kim. Jongha Kim let me know basic paper background(e.g.,Transformer, ViT, CoT, GPT-3, BLIP-2) to understand vision-language models.
Jinyoung Kim taught me how to survey related works and how to pick up and analyze qualitative examples, and gave me chances to write draft on abstract, conclusion, and dataset sections. In addition, he let me know how to use Tmux in Linux for managing multi-GPU setups.
All these chances were thanks to Prof. Hyunwoo J, Kim.
.
After that, the interest in VLMs has expanded to VLA(vision-language-action) model while working as an intern at DAVIAN and UTL Lab.
